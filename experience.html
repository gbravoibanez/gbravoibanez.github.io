Thesis Project: Diaspora and Diglossia: How the Syrian Refugee Crisis has affected Syrian Colloquial Arabic’s Maintenance
September 2020 - May 2021
  • To complete my Global Studies concentration at Greens Farms Academy, I worked on a thesis about Arabic sociolinguistics. I particularly researched how the movement of Syrian refugees into Jordan affected the preservation of Syrian Colloquial Arabic. By conducting interviews with volunteers who have worked closely with Syrian refugees in Jordan, Lebanon, and Turkey and analysing findings from other Arabic sociolinguistic studies, this research concludes that the Syrian dialect is being altered in that linguistic practices are affected by generational differences and exposure to discrimination.
